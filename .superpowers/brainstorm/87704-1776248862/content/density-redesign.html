A

Headlines + one-line preview

Quietest. 6 entries per spread, all peers, all scannable. Closest to the current spread but with truncation and titles. Lowest design risk.

B

Hero + two cards per page

Strong hierarchy. The newest synthesis or nudge is the hero with a pull-quote; secondary entries are quiet cards. Feels designed.

C

Editorial spread

Left page = a feature article (synthesis + nudge). Right page = a clean table of recent entries. Most magazine-like, most visually different from a feed.
