V.

Surfaces

Cards are paper on cream. Lift is rare — only used when a card is being actively interacted with. Bands are how we divide vertical sections without a rule doing the work alone.

Cardpaper · cream · warm · leather
Paper · default
A card on cream, with paper inside.

Used for most content blocks — one practice, one volume preview, one report row.

Cream · inset panel
A slightly warmer inset.

For the "right page" of a spread, or nested content where the reader is going deeper.

Warm · accent block
For moments that want warmth.

A quote card, a today-practice banner, a rare celebration.

Leather · reversed
The Archive's quiet room.

Used for the Archive only, and for the pen when floating over light content.

VI.

Controls

Buttons are rare. Most actions are italic-serif text links with an arrow. Pills are tags, not buttons. The Pen is the global capture — one floating circle, always on the right.

Buttondefault · primary · quiet · sizes

Open Kaleb's volume Read the full synthesis ⟶ Sans variant · 15px
The Penglobal capture · bottom-right, always

Hover the Pen to see the scale feedback. Click it to capture anywhere — a journal entry, an observation, a question. The Pen is the only element that persists across all rooms.
