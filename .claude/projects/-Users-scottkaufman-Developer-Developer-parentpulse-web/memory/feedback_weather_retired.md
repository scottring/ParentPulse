The weather metaphor (sun/clouds/rain for relationship health) was an experiment and is being retired. It didn't hold things together the way the manual/book metaphor does.

**Why:** The manual doesn't have to feel analytical or technical. A leather-bound book can be warm and familial. Weather competed visually (glass morphism + weather animations + paper textures = three layers fighting) and conceptually (analytical manual vs organic weather = two different emotional registers).

**How to apply:** Don't reference weather states, climate engine, or weather animations in new UI work. The book metaphor is the primary design language going forward. See `docs/DESIGN-VISION.md` for the full vision.
